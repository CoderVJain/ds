A Royal Proclamation of Love

Written by Royal Decree in the Language of Kings

♥ ♥ ♥ ♥

To Mine Most Beloved Lady,

By Royal Decree and Divine Providence, I do hereby proclaim mine eternal devotion unto thee, fair maiden of incomparable beauty and grace.

In the presence of the Almighty and before all the Courts of Heaven, I declare that thou art the sovereign of mine heart, the empress of mine soul, and the very essence of mine being.

As the stars in their celestial dance do honour the moon, so doth mine heart honour thee above all earthly treasures. Thy smile doth illuminate the darkest chambers of mine spirit, and thy voice is sweeter than the song of angels.

I do solemnly swear by mine royal blood and sacred honour, to cherish thee with the devotion of a thousand knights, to protect thee with the strength of a thousand armies, and to love thee with the passion of a thousand lifetimes.

Let it be known throughout all the realms that thou art mine chosen queen, mine eternal companion, and the light that guides mine path through the darkness of this world.

From this day forth until the end of time itself, I pledge mine unwavering loyalty, mine boundless affection, and mine very soul unto thee, most precious jewel of mine heart.

By Royal Seal and Sacred Vow,

♥ His Royal Highness, Prince of Thy Heart ♥
